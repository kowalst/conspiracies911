Technical Description

The sources we curated were researched meticulously. We were motivated to answer specific questions we had about 9/11 conspiracy theories. Not only was it important for us to answer questions we had but, it was vital to research our sources to validate their accuracy and efficacy. While processing the data in the WayBack Machine we used multiple tools. Part of this project was giving data visualizations to make the data easier to understand and to pull trends or assumptions from the data set. The presentation of our findings was the exciting part of this project. Bringing everything we learned together in one place and making the data, trends, research, and findings more accessible to an audience was time-consuming but, rewarding.
